Advertising tricks

Do not believe every healthy-looking package.

Companies use colours, words, characters, and small details to make products seem healthier than they are.

Package trick examples

Real packages can try to steer your choices.

Look at the picture first, then read the caption to see what trick the package might be using.

1

Health words

Natural, real, energy, or made with fruit can still hide sugar or salt.

2

Package design

Green colours, fruit pictures, or athletes can make food feel healthier.

3

Small serving sizes

A label can look better when the serving size is tiny.
