Meal ideas

Choose meals that ads cannot fake.

Build snacks and meals from real food groups instead of slogans.

2-group snack

Apple slices with yogurt, carrots with hummus, or whole grain crackers with cheese.

3-group breakfast

Whole grain toast, egg, and fruit, or oatmeal with berries and milk.

4-group lunch or dinner

Rice or pasta, chicken or beans, vegetables, and milk or yogurt.
