Smart eating tips

Use this quick system before you buy.

These tips make the website easier to remember when you are looking at real food packages.

1

Front words are not proof

Words like natural, light, energy, protein, or made with fruit can sound healthy. Always check the facts.

2

Serving size changes everything

If you eat double the serving, you also double the sugar, sodium, calories, and other numbers.

3

Ingredients tell the truth

Ingredients are listed by amount. If sugar, syrup, oil, or refined flour is first, pay attention.

4

Use the Food Guide plate

A strong meal has vegetables and fruits, whole grains, protein foods, and water most often.

5

Ask who benefits

An ad wants you to buy. Your job is to slow down and ask what the package is trying to make you feel.

6

Use the 2-3-4 Challenge

Try 2 food groups for snacks, 3 for breakfast, and 4 for lunch or dinner.
