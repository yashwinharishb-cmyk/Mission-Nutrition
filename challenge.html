The challenge

A simple plan beats brand promises.

The 2-3-4 Challenge means 2 food groups for snacks, 3 for breakfast, and 4 for lunch and dinner.

1) 2 Food Groups

Snacks

Fruit with yogurt, vegetables with hummus, or crackers with cheese.

2) 3 Food Groups

Breakfast

Whole grain toast, an egg, and fruit, or oatmeal with milk and berries.

3) 4 Food Groups

Lunch & dinner

Rice, beans or chicken, vegetables, and milk or yogurt.
